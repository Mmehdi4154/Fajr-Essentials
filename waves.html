Full Ingredient List

How to Use

  1. Wet your hands or body with warm water.
  2. Lather the soap bar between your palms to create a rich foam.
  3. Apply gently in circular motions over skin. Avoid the eye area.
  4. Rinse thoroughly with water and pat dry.
  5. Follow with your favourite moisturiser for best results.

For best preservation, store the soap on a well‑draining soap dish away from direct water.
